1st Xinao Cup

The Xinao Cup (新奥杯, also called the ENN Cup) is a tournament that was held only once, with preliminaries in 2016 and final in 2017.

Chinese rules are followed. In particular, komi is 7.5. Time allotted is 2h in the preliminary rounds, 2.5h in the main tournament.

The main tournament has 64 participants, of whom 14 were seeded (including the winner of the Taiwanese Preliminary).

Preliminaries

See gotoeveryone or yigo. (There are 23 sgfs from the preliminaries here.) The Women's Preliminaries were won by Cao Youyin (3p) and Zhang Xuan (8p). The Senior's Preliminaries were won by Yu Bin (9p) and Fang Tianfeng (8p). The Non-Asian Preliminaries were won by Jiang Zhujiu (9p). Each of these lost in the 1st round, except for Fang Tianfeng, who reached the 3rd round.

Taiwanese Preliminary

See here. The winner was Wang Yuanjun (7p), who lost in the 1st round.
